# ⚖️ Professional Practice & Ethics (CS-490)

Welcome to the **Professional Practice & Ethics (PPE)** course directory! This folder archives core course outlines, professional ethics guidebooks, Harvard Business Review (HBR) communication manuals, career development assets, and capstone venture presentations completed during Semester 8.

This course bridges the gap between software development and professional corporate practice, instilling principles of computing ethics, intellectual property rights, executive communication, interview performance, and responsible AI system architecture.

## 🧠 Core Competencies Achieved

### 1. Software Engineering Ethics & Law
*   **Codes of Professional Conduct**: Applying IEEE/ACM ethical standards to software safety, privacy, and intellectual property.
*   **Software Licensing**: Differentiating Open Source (MIT, GPL, Apache) vs. Proprietary software frameworks.

### 2. High-Impact Professional Communication
*   **HBS Business Writing**: Structuring concise, persuasive, and clear technical proposals, reports, and emails.
*   **Executive Storytelling**: Preparing technical slide decks for non-technical stakeholders and executive boards.

### 3. Career Positioning & Interview Mastery
*   **Personal Branding**: Formulating a unique value proposition as a CS graduate and AI developer.
*   **Behavioral & Technical Interviews**: Applying STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) response techniques to complex technical questions.
