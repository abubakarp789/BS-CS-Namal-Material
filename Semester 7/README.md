## 🌟 Semester Learning Objectives

Through these courses, I have gained hands-on expertise in the following domains:

1. **Deep Mathematical Modeling**:
   - Programmed and debugged Machine Learning mathematical models (Multivariate Linear Regression, LDA matrices, PCA covariance, and LSTM backpropagation matrices) without the abstraction of scikit-learn or deep learning frameworks.
2. **High-Performance Architecture & Operations**:
   - Deployed high-performance containerized microservices behind a custom Nginx reverse proxy load-balancer, analyzing scalability dimensions, load distribution, and Amdahl's Law execution logs.
3. **Offensive & Defensive Security**:
   - Exploited standard web vulnerabilities (SQLi, XSS, CSRF) using Burp Suite in safe sandboxed environments, examined spoofing mechanics (ARP, DHCP, DNS), and reviewed PKI architectures for Digital Signatures.
4. **Venture Capital & Software Planning**:
   - Modeled an AI software startup (SudhaarAI) from concept to execution: authored a full SRS document and a VC-pitchable Business Plan covering monetization, financial mapping, and competitive analysis.
5. **Humanities & Philosophical Critical Thinking**:
   - Engaged in critical exploration of eastern philosophical constructs and did practical field community initiatives based on educational and motivational poems.
