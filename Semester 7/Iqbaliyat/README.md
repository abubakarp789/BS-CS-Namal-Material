# 📖 Iqbaliyat (SS-102)

Welcome to the **Iqbaliyat** course directory! This folder archives research assignments, lecture presentations, and a practical social-philosophical field project completed during Semester 7. 

Iqbaliyat focuses on the literary, philosophical, and educational concepts of **Dr. Allama Muhammad Iqbal**, particularly exploring the concepts of **Khudi (Selfhood)**, **Shaheen (The Falcon/Visionary Youth)**, **Intellect and Love (Aqal-o-Ishq)**, and the reconstruction of religious thought in modern times.

## 🕌 Featured Practical Project: Tariq ki Dua Study

The major highlight of this course was a practical community field project titled **"Tariq ki Dua"**:

> [!NOTE]
> *"Tariq ki Dua"* is a powerful poem in Bal-e-Jibril representing the prayer of Tariq bin Ziyad (the Muslim conqueror of Spain). It instills themes of courage, absolute faith, spiritual determination, and selflessness in youth.

### Project Deliverables:
1.  **Field Report**: Detailing the educational sessions conducted with regional youth to spark discussion on Iqbal's concepts of self-sufficiency.
2.  **Visual Evidence & Certificate**: PNG files capturing community engagement, school presentations, and the final course project validation certificate.

## 🎓 Core Themes Explored

*   **Khudi (The Self)**: Developing self-awareness, moral integrity, and reliance on inner spiritual energy.
*   **Aqal-o-Ishq (Intellect vs. Love/Passion)**: Balancing scientific research and objective logic with deep passion, ethics, and empathy.
*   **The Concept of Shaheen**: Inspiring the younger generation to fly high, explore new intellectual frontiers, and remain independent of materialistic constraints.
